# Sentio — Product Overview

## What Sentio Does

Sentio is an AI-powered customer health scoring and churn prediction platform built for B2B SaaS companies. It connects to a company's existing data sources — product usage, support tickets, NPS/CSAT responses, billing events, and stakeholder engagement — and synthesizes them into a single, real-time health score per account.

**Core value proposition:** Sentio gives CS teams a 60–90 day warning before an account is likely to churn, with a recommended action attached — not just a red flag.

When an account's health score crosses a risk threshold, Sentio surfaces a recommended playbook (e.g., executive outreach, discount offer, onboarding re-engagement) and assigns it to the right CSM automatically.

---

## The Three Problems Sentio Solves

### 1. CS Blind Spots
CS teams manage 50–150 accounts each and can't monitor every signal manually. Product usage drops, support ticket spikes, NPS score changes, billing failures — these signals exist in five different tools and no one is watching all of them at once. Sentio aggregates every signal automatically so nothing slips through.

### 2. Reactive Customer Success
Most CS teams learn about churn risk from the customer — after the damage is done. A CSM hears "we're not renewing" on the renewal call, not 60 days before it. Sentio inverts that: the CSM reaches out before the customer has a complaint, because Sentio surfaced the risk first.

### 3. Inconsistent Playbooks
Different CSMs handle at-risk accounts differently. A senior CSM with good instincts escalates early. A newer CSM waits. The outcome varies by rep, not by account health. Sentio standardizes escalation logic so the best-performing response becomes the team's default, regardless of who owns the account.

## Key Differentiators

- **Predictive, not descriptive:** Sentio doesn't just show you what happened — it tells you what's likely to happen next and when.
- **Recommended actions, not just alerts:** Every risk flag comes with a suggested next step (playbook) assigned to the right CSM.
- **Connects in under a day:** No custom ETL, no data engineering project. Sentio integrates with the tools you already use — Salesforce, HubSpot, Amplitude, Intercom, Stripe — and starts scoring accounts within 24 hours.
- **Built for mid-market CS teams:** Designed for companies with 100–800 employees and 5–30 CSMs. Not an enterprise suite. Not a spreadsheet.

---

## Who Uses Sentio

Sentio is used by B2B SaaS companies with $5M–$50M ARR that have a dedicated Customer Success function. The primary users are:

- **VP / Director of Customer Success** — owns the health scoring dashboard, renewal forecast, and playbook assignments
- **CS Managers and CSMs** — receive daily task queues with prioritized account actions
- **CRO / CFO** — reviews NRR impact and retention metrics in the executive dashboard
