# Sentio — Pricing Tiers

> All pricing below is fictional and illustrative only.

---

## Overview

Sentio offers three tiers: Starter, Growth, and Enterprise. All plans are billed annually. Monthly billing is available on Starter at a 20% premium.

| | Starter | Growth | Enterprise |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Price (annual)** | $1,500/mo · $18,000/yr | $3,000/mo · $36,000/yr | Custom (typically $60,000+/yr) |
| **Price (monthly)** | $1,800/mo | Annual only | Annual only |
| **CSM seats** | Up to 3 | Up to 10 | Unlimited |
| **Accounts monitored** | Up to 200 | Up to 1,000 | Unlimited |

---

## Starter — $18,000/year

**Best for:** Early-stage CS teams (2–3 CSMs) that want health scoring without a full platform investment.

**Included:**
- Health scoring engine (product usage + support signals)
- 1 CRM integration (HubSpot or Salesforce — not both)
- 1 support integration (Intercom)
- Pre-built playbooks (5 built-in templates)
- CSM daily task queue
- Email-only support (response within 2 business days)

**Not included on Starter:**
- NPS/CSAT signal ingestion
- Billing event monitoring
- Custom playbook builder
- Renewal forecast dashboard
- API access
- Slack support or dedicated CSM

**Upgrade trigger:** Exceeding 3 CSM seats, needing a second CRM integration, or requiring the renewal forecast dashboard.

---

## Growth — $36,000/year

**Best for:** Mid-market CS teams (4–10 CSMs) where churn is a board-level metric and the CS team needs a full operational platform.

**Included:**
- Everything in Starter, plus:
- Up to 10 CSM seats
- Up to 1,000 accounts monitored
- Full integration suite: HubSpot + Salesforce + Segment + Amplitude (product analytics)
- NPS/CSAT signal ingestion (Delighted, Medallia, Typeform)
- Billing event monitoring (Stripe, Chargebee, Recurly)
- Custom playbook builder
- Renewal forecast dashboard
- Segment-level health insights
- REST API access
- Slack + email support
- Shared CSM (office hours, quarterly check-in)
- Champion monitoring (job change detection)

**This is Sentio's most popular tier** for companies with $5M–$30M ARR and 100–400 employees.

**Upgrade trigger:** Exceeding 10 CSM seats, needing unlimited accounts, requiring a dedicated CSM or SLA, or needing SSO/SAML for enterprise security review.

---

## Enterprise — Custom Pricing (typically $60,000+/year)

**Best for:** Companies with large CS teams (10+ CSMs), complex security requirements, or the need for custom integrations and SLAs.

**Included:**
- Everything in Growth, plus:
- Unlimited CSM seats
- Unlimited accounts monitored
- Custom data connectors (for non-standard internal data sources)
- SSO / SAML authentication
- Data residency options (US, EU)
- Dedicated CSM (named, quarterly business reviews)
- 4-hour SLA on critical support issues
- Custom onboarding and implementation support
- Executive reporting package

**Pricing:** Based on ARR, number of accounts, and integration complexity. Average deal size $60,000–$120,000/year.

## Frequently Asked Questions on Pricing

**Is there a free trial?**
Yes — Sentio offers a 30-day pilot for qualified Growth and Enterprise prospects. The pilot connects to one integration, monitors up to 50 accounts, and includes a full onboarding session. Pilots are offered after a discovery call, not self-serve.

**Can I start on Starter and upgrade to Growth?**
Yes. Upgrades are prorated to your remaining contract term. Most teams that start on Starter upgrade to Growth within 6 months when their CS team grows past 3 seats or they want the renewal forecast dashboard.

**What counts as a "CSM seat"?**
Anyone who logs into Sentio to manage accounts or view task queues counts as a seat. Read-only dashboard access for managers or executives does not consume a seat.

**Are there setup fees?**
No setup fees on Starter or Growth. Enterprise plans may include a one-time onboarding fee for custom integration work — this is scoped during the sales process.

**Is annual billing required?**
Growth and Enterprise are annual only. Starter can be billed monthly at $1,800/month.

**What's the contract length?**
Starter: month-to-month or annual. Growth and Enterprise: 12-month minimum. Multi-year discounts available (typically 10–15% for 2-year, 20% for 3-year).

**Do you offer nonprofit or startup discounts?**
Contact the sales team. Sentio has a startup program for pre-Series A companies — typically Starter tier at a reduced rate with an upgrade commitment tied to a funding milestone.
