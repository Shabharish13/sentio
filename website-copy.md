### Plans

#### Starter
- **Price:** $1,500 /mo · $18K/yr

- **Seats:** Up to 3 CSMs
- **Integrations:** HubSpot or Salesforce (1) + Intercom

- **CTA:** Start with Starter

#### Growth — *Most popular*
- **Price:** $3,000 /mo · $36K/yr
- **Blurb:** The sweet spot for 100–300 person SaaS teams scaling NRR.
- **Seats:** Up to 10 CSMs
- **Integrations:** HubSpot + Salesforce + Segment + Amplitude
- **Support:** Slack + email, shared CSM
- **CTA:** Request a demo

#### Enterprise
- **Price:** Custom · $60K+/yr
- **Blurb:** For complex orgs needing custom connectors, SSO, and a dedicated CSM.
- **Seats:** Unlimited CSMs
- **Integrations:** Full stack + custom connectors + SSO/SAML
- **Support:** Dedicated CSM, SLA
- **CTA:** Talk to sales

**Below plans:** Not sure which plan fits? The assistant in the corner can help — or just book a demo.

### Pricing FAQ

**What triggers an upgrade?**
Most teams move from Starter to Growth when they add a fourth CSM or need more than one CRM/analytics integration. Growth to Enterprise is usually driven by SSO/SAML, custom connectors, or an SLA requirement.

**Is there an annual discount?**
Yes — annual pricing is shown above (roughly two months free versus monthly). Talk to us for multi-year terms.

**How long does onboarding take?**
Sentio connects to your existing stack in under a day. There's no custom ETL to build.
