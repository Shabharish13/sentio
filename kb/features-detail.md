# Sentio — Features Detail

## Health Scoring Engine

Sentio's core feature is a per-account health score that updates continuously as new signals arrive.

**What feeds the health score:**
- **Product usage** — login frequency, feature adoption depth, time-in-app trends, DAU/MAU ratio
- **Support activity** — open ticket count, ticket severity, resolution time, escalation rate
- **NPS / CSAT signals** — score trends over time, detractor flag, survey non-response (treated as a weak negative signal)
- **Billing events** — payment failures, downgrade requests, plan pauses
- **Stakeholder engagement** — email open rates, meeting cadence, champion contact recency
- **Renewal proximity** — risk weighting increases as renewal date approaches

**Score output:** A 0–100 health score per account, updated in real time. Thresholds (configurable per tier):
- 70–100: Healthy
- 40–69: At-Risk
- 0–39: Critical

---

## Churn Prediction Model

On top of the health score, Sentio runs a churn prediction model trained on historical account behavior patterns. It outputs a churn probability (0–100%) for each account at a rolling 90-day horizon.

The model identifies leading indicators — not just lagging signals. Examples:
- A drop in product login frequency 90 days before renewal is a stronger churn signal than at 30 days.
- A champion leaving the company (detected via LinkedIn job change monitoring) is flagged as a high-risk event regardless of the health score.
- A support ticket marked "frustrated" or "considering alternatives" in free-text triggers a sentiment flag.

---

## Playbook Engine

When an account crosses a risk threshold or a churn signal fires, Sentio automatically surfaces a recommended playbook. Playbooks are pre-built or custom-configured by the CS team.

**Example built-in playbooks:**
- **Executive Outreach** — for accounts where the champion has gone quiet; triggers a task to schedule a call with the economic buyer
- **Onboarding Re-engagement** — for accounts that adopted 2 or fewer features in the first 60 days; triggers a walkthrough session invitation
- **Discount Offer** — for price-sensitive accounts with a price objection flag in support tickets; triggers an AE review task
- **Health Check Call** — default playbook for accounts entering the At-Risk band; assigns the CSM a follow-up task within 48 hours

Playbooks are assigned to the account owner (CSM) automatically. CS managers can configure which playbook fires for which threshold.

---

## CSM Daily Task Queue

Each CSM starts their day with a prioritized task list generated by Sentio. Tasks are ranked by:
1. Renewal proximity × churn probability
2. Playbook urgency level
3. Days since last outreach

No spreadsheet. No cross-referencing five dashboards. One view of what to do today and why.

---

## Renewal Forecast Dashboard

Sentio provides a renewal forecast view for CS leaders and CROs. It shows:
- **At-risk ARR** — total ARR across accounts in the At-Risk and Critical bands
- **Likely to churn** — accounts where churn probability exceeds 60% at the 90-day horizon
- **NRR projection** — rolling 90-day net revenue retention estimate
- **Expansion opportunities** — accounts with high health scores and low feature adoption (upsell signal)

This view replaces the "gut-feel renewal call" in most CS org QBRs.

---

## Champion Monitoring

Sentio monitors for job changes among key contacts at each account (using LinkedIn public data and email bounce detection). When a champion leaves, Sentio:
- Flags the account as "champion risk"
- Triggers a playbook to identify and engage a new internal champion
- Notifies the CSM and AE

Champion exits are one of the strongest leading indicators of churn and are typically invisible until it's too late.

---

## Segment-Level Insights

CS leaders can view health trends aggregated by:
- Industry segment
- Cohort (by contract start date)
- Product tier
- CSM (to identify performance variance)
- Geography

Useful for identifying systemic issues — e.g., all accounts that onboarded in Q3 have a 30% lower health score than Q4 cohorts — that wouldn't be visible at the account level.

## API Access

Growth and Enterprise tiers include REST API access for:
- Pulling health scores and churn probabilities into your internal BI tools
- Pushing custom events into Sentio's scoring engine (e.g., from internal product data not connected via native integration)
- Triggering playbooks programmatically from external systems

API documentation available to customers post-onboarding.
